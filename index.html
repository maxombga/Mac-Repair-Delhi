MacBook Logic Board Repair


When it comes to the functionality of your MacBook, the logic board plays a crucial role. As the main circuit board that connects and controls all the essential components of your device, any issues with the logic board can lead to various performance problems. From sudden crashes and freezing to malfunctioning peripherals and failure to boot, logic board problems can be frustrating and disruptive. At MakCity, we understand the significance of a properly functioning logic board, and our expert technicians are equipped to diagnose and repair any logic board issues your MacBook may be experiencing.

What's the price for MacBook Logic Board Repair?

The cost of repairing a MacBook's logic board can vary significantly based on several factors. Generally, the expense can range from around ₹1500 to ₹20000 or more. This variation depends on factors like the specific model of MacBook, the extent of the damage to the logic board, the availability of replacement parts, and whether the repair is being done by an authorized service provider or a third-party repair shop.
Smaller issues like simple component replacements might fall on the lower end of the price range. However, more complex problems that require intricate repairs or component-level diagnostics can drive the cost higher.

Signs that Your MacBook Needs Logic Board Repair

Recognizing the signs that indicate your MacBook may need logic board repair can save you from further complications. Common symptoms include the dreaded "blue screen of death," random shutdowns, failure to turn on, unresponsive keyboard or trackpad, and frequent kernel panics. Additionally, if you've spilled liquid on your MacBook or experienced a power surge, it's essential to have the logic board inspected by professionals to prevent potential damage.

How can I identify if my MacBook logic board needs repair?
If your MacBook experiences random shutdowns, overheating, no power, or exhibits strange behavior like booting issues, it might indicate a problem with the logic board. Additionally, if your MacBook displays a blank screen or shows specific error messages related to hardware, it's advisable to seek logic board repair services.
Can MacBook logic board be repaired?
Logic Boards can indeed be repaired if the technicians have the necessary expertise to handle Mac's hardware. At MakCity, our technicians have the required skillset to fix any issue with your Mac.
Is logic board repair cost-effective compared to buying a new MacBook?
Logic board repair can be a cost-effective solution, especially for older MacBook models or when the issue is specific to the logic board itself. Repairing the logic board is generally more budget-friendly than purchasing a new MacBook, and it allows you to retain your data and settings.
